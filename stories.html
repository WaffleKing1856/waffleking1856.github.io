Dumpster Prime

This is a story about me and my friend Mclean (seen in first image on right). Being poor tech schoolers we were going to throw away our trash at the dumpster.

Upon arrival to the dumpster we found an unopened case of prime in the dumpster. Being the good people that we are, we decided to return it to the owner. Just kidding we stole the dumpster prime, and drank it with a smile on our face.

The Coolest Moment in My Life

After a few White Claws and enough liquid courage, I decided to attempt something I'd always wanted to try - a backflip into the pool. Despite zero gymnastics experience and questionable judgment, I somehow managed to rotate all the way around and (mostly) stick the landing.

My back wasn't too happy with me the next day, but it was worth it for the rush and the bragging rights. Now I can officially say I've done a backflip - even if it was more "send it" style than Olympic form.

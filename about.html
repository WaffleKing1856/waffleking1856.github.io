About Matt

The Work Life

Born and raised on Cape Cod, Matt's career journey started in the classic Cape fashion - as a dishwasher and cook in a busy kitchen. Like many Cape kids, he learned early on about the value of hard work and dealing with summer tourists.

Trading kitchen heat for outdoor grit, he moved on to B and B Excavation, where he spent his days digging trenches and learning the ins and outs of construction work. It was a change of pace that taught him there's more than one way to build something from the ground up.

These days, you'll find him serving in the military - though ironically, he spends most of his time behind a desk. It's quite the journey from washing dishes to defending freedom (from a very comfortable office chair).
